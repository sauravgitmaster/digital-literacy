Do’s:

? Think before you post—consider long-term impact
? Maintain professionalism in your language and content
? Respect others’ opinions and engage politely
? Use privacy settings to control personal information
? Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news)

Don’ts:

? Do not share sensitive personal or academic information
? Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or controversial content
? Do not engage in online arguments or trolling
? Avoid oversharing personal life details
? Do not use social media irresponsibly during academic hours
